// 06. RESEARCH
Publications
ICTACT Journal on Communication Technology — Sep 2020 • Vol. 11, Issue 03 • pp. 2222–2227
Beamforming with Massive MIMO Systems in 5G — Reduction of Beamwidth and Sidelobes
Investigated beamforming techniques for Massive MIMO in 5G networks, analyzing reduction of beamwidth and sidelobe levels using Uniform Linear Arrays (ULA) and Uniform Rectangular Arrays (URA). Demonstrated that increasing antenna count produces a narrower, more directed pencil beam — with 64-antenna arrays showing significantly improved directivity. Explored 3D beam pattern optimization for power, electric field, and directivity in multi-antenna configurations.
